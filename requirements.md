# Requirements

Created 20151226 by [Damir Cavar]



Required compiler and environment:

* [G++] and [GCC], or [clang], or any other [C++] compiler with [C++11] suport
* [CMake]
* [BNFC]
* [Boost] libraries


Required for the morphologies:

* [Foma]
