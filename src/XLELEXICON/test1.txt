
"ADJECTIVE TAGS"

Ma+ 	  ADJPFX XLE. "ma- prefix forming adjectives from nouns or verbs"

"Adjectives"

"Some adjectives are lexically specified as having an adverbial use, so we must allow for this here."

+Adj 	  ADJTAG XLE { (^ CHECK _MORPH _WORDTYPE) = adj
		     | (^ CHECK _MORPH _WORDTYPE) = adv }.
