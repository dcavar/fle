
"Prepositions, adverbs, and locative deictics taking a tense prefix
and/or pronominal object suffixes go through the morphological
analyzer and appear here.  Invariant adverbs and prepositions 
are in the indeclinable lexicon file."

"OBLIQUE TAGS"

PresentTense+ +OPERF XLE @(PREP-TENSE pres); "Tense prefix for prepositions."
 ETC. 

PastTense+ +OPERF XLE @(PREP-TENSE past); "Tense prefix."
 ETC. 

NonpastTense+ OPERF XLE (^ TNS-ASP TENSE) ~= past "null marking, default present"
			@(DEFAULT (^ TNS-ASP TENSE) pres); 
 ETC.

Directive+ DEICTIC_DIR XLE @(SUBJ-OBLloc motion). "Verbalizing prefix for deictics."

Locative+ OLOC XLE @(PREP directional). "Locative prefix for locative deictics (from here, to there)"

+Deictic  DEICTIC_TAG XLE. "Closed-class locative deictics."

+Oblique  OBL_TAG XLE. "Adverbs and prepositions."

"PREPOSITION AND ADVERB ROOTS"

"Irregular tensed prepositions"

mandritra +Prep XLE @(PREP-NOM during)
		    @(PREP-TENSE pres);
	  ETC.

nandritra +Prep XLE @(PREP-NOM during)
		    @(PREP-TENSE past); 
	  ETC.


"Prepositions taking genitive objects.  Some of the following 
take t- prefix for past tense.  Others do not alternate for tense."

"Prepositions taking genitive objects or objects with another case."

amina 	  +Prep XLE @(PREP-GEN generic_prep)
		    @(PSEM generic)
		    @(OT-MARK closed-class);
	  +Prep XLE @(PREP-ACC generic_prep)
		    @(PSEM generic)
		    @(OT-MARK closed-class);
	  ETC.

hatramina +P * @(PREP-GEN from_or_up_to);
	  +P * @(PREP-NOM including);
	  ETC.

tahaka 	  +P * @(PREP-GEN like);

	  ETC.
